The art of hadicragt in Venice is as unique and passionate as it wascenturies ago. You can savour a Venice of almost another era, discovering the secrets of master crafrsmen that will create under your eyes the "pearls" of Venice's top handcrft tradition: velvets, brocades, books, frames, furnishings, leather bindings, Carnival masks and other wonders.

The Lagoon's Islands

Thinking that Venice itself is an archipelago, with its 118 islands interconnected over time by more than 400 bridges, is still remarkable. But the outer island of the Lagoon offer some of the most fascinating spots of the entire area. In addition to Murano, renowned glass- making centre, and Burano, famous for its colourful painted houses, other islands are worth a visit Sant'Erasmo -a vegtable garden since "Serenissima" times- or Mazzorbo, with its few brightly- painted houses 'sprinkled' among the vineyards, fruit orchards and vegetable fields.
